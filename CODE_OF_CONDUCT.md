# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Summary

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive experience for everyone. We expect all participants to:

- Be respectful and constructive in communication
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy toward other community members

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue at [github.com/sylorei/claude-godmode/issues](https://github.com/sylorei/claude-godmode/issues) or contacting the maintainer directly.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The maintainer is obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident.

For the full text, see the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
