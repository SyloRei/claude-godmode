<objective>
Close CR-01 from `05-VERIFICATION.md`: the vocabulary gate (`scripts/check-vocab.sh`)
ships green-mechanically but the corpus has 18 unallowlisted `phase`/`milestone`
references in 6 SKILL.md bodies. Without this fix, the CI vocab job is RED on the
first push to main after the Phase 5 PR merges. SC #1 + SC #4 cannot pass.

Strategy chosen: **scrub-the-skills** for `Phase N`-style dev-side cross-references
(they leaked from internal context into user-facing prose by accident), and
**extend-the-allowlist** for `mission/SKILL.md`'s legitimate use of `milestone`
(the v2 chain word for the level above brief — see CLAUDE.md
"Project → Mission → Brief → Plan → Commit").

Why this split, not all-allowlist:
- `Phase N` references are NOT user-facing concepts — the v2 product talks about
  briefs and milestones, never phases. Allowlisting would entrench dev-side
  leakage in the public skill bodies.
- `milestone` IS a v2 user-facing concept (PROJECT.md / CLAUDE.md), and only
  `mission/SKILL.md` legitimately surfaces it. A scoped allowlist matches
  reality without weakening the gate.

Purpose: ship CI green on first merge to main; preserve the gate's actual contract.
Output: 6 SKILL.md edits (in-place neutralizations) + 1 `check-vocab.sh` allowlist
extension (mission-only `milestone` exemption).
</objective>
