## Codebase Patterns
- Skills are defined as Markdown files with YAML frontmatter in skills/[name]/SKILL.md
- Pipeline artifacts live under .claude-pipeline/ subdirectories (PRDs in .claude-pipeline/prds/)
- Quality gates: shell syntax check on hooks/*.sh, grep-based checks for stale references
- The grep quality gate excludes Claude tool names (TaskCreate, TaskUpdate, etc.) and "task tools"/"multi-step tasks" phrases
